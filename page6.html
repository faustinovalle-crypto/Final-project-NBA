Stephen Curry

About Steph

Stephen Curry is an American professional basketball player for the Golden State Warriors, born March 14, 1988, in Akron, Ohio. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest shooters in NBA history, known for revolutionizing the game with his three-point shooting and leading the Warriors to multiple championships. A two-time NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP) and a four-time NBA champion, Curry also has numerous other accolades and holds several NBA records.
