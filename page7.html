About Jordan

Michael Jordan is a former American professional basketball player, widely regarded as the greatest of all time, who won six NBA championships with the Chicago Bulls in the 1990s. After his career, he became a successful businessman, and is the owner of the Charlotte Hornets and a minority owner of the NASCAR team 23XI Racing.
