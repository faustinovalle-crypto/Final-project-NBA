Larry Bird

About Larry

Larry Bird is a former professional basketball player, coach, and executive known for his legendary career with the Boston Celtics. After leading Indiana State University to the NCAA finals, he was drafted by the Celtics in 1978 and went on to win three NBA championships and three consecutive MVP awards. He is widely considered one of the greatest basketball players of all time and later found success in coaching and executive roles with the Indiana Pacers.
